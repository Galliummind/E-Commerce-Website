Warranty

Peace of mind is as serious to us as it is to you. That's why we are proud to offer our 10 day guarantee against DoA.

Any product we sell comes with a guaranteed protection against DoA (Dead on Arrival). That means you can rest knowing your electronics will reach you safely and you always have great customer support. If you want some extra coverage check out our 1-year or 2-year warranty.

How does it work?

If a product needs to be returned because it is defective, all you will need to do is return the electronic with the receipt to your local dealer. If our local technicians cannot repair the item or it requires more than 5 business days to repair we will replace the defective item or give you %100 percent money back. But what if I want a full year warranty? This can be done by taking advantage of our extended warranty. You can read the details below.

Extended Warranty

By purchasing the extended warranty you are covered for 1-year or 2-years against any defects. The same rules apply as the extended warranty. If you're into peace of mind it can't get any better than using our peace of mind.

Not Covered Under Warranty

  • Liquid damage.
  • Loss of data
  • Theft
  • Damage caused modifications
  • Damage caused by nature (ex. earthquake, tsunami)
  • Unusual abuse to a product (laptops make poor frisbies)
