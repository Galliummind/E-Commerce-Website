One Man's Trash is Another Man's Treasure

Did you know that over 40 million metric tons of waste is produced each year! A lot of these electronics work, but in the fast-paced world of electronics they are deemed "out-dated". A good portion of these electronics can make a huge impact on someone. Messer computers first started by recyling that electronic waste as a volunteering project. Over the course of six years we managed to provide free computers for many people based on their needs. This was done by taking the time to carefully understand each persons needs and pairing them with a computer that suited them. By focusing on customer needs we could determine who needed the freshly built gaming PC and who only needed the reliable desktop computer. This helped build Messer Computer's code of ethics

"We will always act in your best interest"
